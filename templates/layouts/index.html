IronJacamar

Powering the connectivity layer for WildFly, IronJacamar is a high-performance implementation of the Jakarta Connectors 2.1 specification. It provides the architectural glue between the Jakarta EE Platform and Enterprise Information Systems (EIS). Whether you’re bridging databases, message brokers, or legacy mainframes, IronJacamar handles the heavy lifting of resource adapter lifecycle and contract management.
